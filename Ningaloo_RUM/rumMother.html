Data

Data for recreational marine use in Esperance was collected through face-to-face surveys at boat launches. The surveys facilitated the collection of multi-use data and multi-greement data.The survey had a 90% response rate with 1152 participants, of those 1077 survey were first instance or single agreement surveys and 75 were multi-agreement surveys (survey participant surveyed for a second or more times). This produced a total of 1481 spatial use data points, 329 of which were part of multi-use trips (excluding first instance).
